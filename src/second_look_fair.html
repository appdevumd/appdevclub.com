Overview

If you were with us last semester, you know that we were primarily a workshop-based club. However, this semester, we decided to do something a little different. App Dev Club is now going to be entirely project-based, and members will be able to come up with their own project ideas without restriction and implement them (in groups or individually - you choose).

Communication

As like most other clubs here on campus, communication will be primarily through Discord, so make sure to join our server! Also, our main website can be found here.

Projects and Technologies

If you happen to need assistance or mentorship in your project, we most likely got you covered! App Dev Club has tech leads who are familiar with React Native, Flutter, Swift, React, and backend development in Node and Django and are committed to making sure every project gets to completion. If you decide to use a framework or technology that was not mentioned earlier, we will do our best to help you out as well.

Workshops

Last but not least, we are not removing workshops entirely. We are just removing most of them and carefully choosing the remaining ones so that they are as relevant and useful to members as possible.
